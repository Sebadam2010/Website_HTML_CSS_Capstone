Useful Websites

  • OpenPowerlifting
    The OpenPowerlifting project aims to create a permanent, accurate, convenient, accessible, open archive of the world's powerlifting data.
  • KiloCalc
    Kilocalc allows users to accurately load the barbell based on the plates that are available with advanced configuration settings.
  • OpenLifter
    OpenLifter is powerlifting meet software that runs blazingly fast, works everywhere, and is a joy to use. Featuring next-up bar loading, full offline functionality, instant, exportable results, and more.
  • Depth Captcha
    Depth Captcha is a simple educational game about squat depth.
  • Strength Level
    Strength Level exists to make you stronger by helping you to understand how strong you are compared to other lifters.
